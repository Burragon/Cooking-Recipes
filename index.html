Peel and slice the apples into small chunks

Slice the onion

Add the butter/oil to the saucepan and melt it

Add the onion slices and cook until soft (5 min, med heat)

Add the water, apple slices, and cinnamon and cook until soft (10 min)

Add 2 more tbsp of water as needed

Add chicken breast tenders and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of garlic powder

< Brown both sides (10 min)

Mix the cornstarch and white wine in a glass and add to the chicken

Cover pan and cook on low-med heat for 10+ min
